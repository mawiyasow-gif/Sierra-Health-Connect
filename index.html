💡 HEALTHY LIVING

Health Tips For EveryDay Wellness

Simple, practical steps you can take every day to protect your health and the health of your family. family.

🍱
Nutrition

Eat a Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your daily meals. Proper nutrition strengthens your immune system and prevents chronic diseases

💧
Wellness

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of clean, safe water daily. Proper hydration is essential for body function and helps prevent heat-related illnesses.

🏋️
Fitness

Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity most days. Walking, dancing, or traditional games all count toward your daily exercise.

💤
Wellness

Get Enough Sleep

Adults should aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night. Good sleep supports mental health, immune function, and overall wellbeing.

💆
Prevention

Practice Good Hygiene

Wash your hands regularly with soap and clean water, especially before eating and after using the toilet. This simple habit prevents many infectious diseases.

💆
Prevention

Stay Current on Vaccinations

Ensure you and your children receive all recommended vaccines according to the national immunization schedule to prevent serious diseases.
