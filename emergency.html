Quick Emergency Guidance Cards

❤️

Heart Attack / Chest Pain

  1. Call 117 immediately
  2. Keep the person calm and seated
  3. Do not give food or drink
  4. Loosen tight clothing
  5. If unconscious, begin CPR if trained
🩸

Severe Bleeding

  1. Call 117 or go to nearest hospital
  2. Apply firm pressure with clean cloth
  3. Keep the injured area elevated
  4. Do not remove embedded objects
  5. Keep the person warm and calm
🫁

Difficulty Breathing

  1. Call 117 immediately
  2. Help the person sit upright
  3. Loosen any tight clothing
  4. Open windows for fresh air
  5. Do not lay the person flat
🌡️

Severe Fever / Convulsions

  1. Call 117 or rush to hospital
  2. Remove excess clothing
  3. Apply cool cloth to forehead
  4. Do not put anything in the mouth
  5. Place person on their side
